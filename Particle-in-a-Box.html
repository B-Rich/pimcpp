For a particle in a box with periodic boundary conditions, the

quantized energy levels in a cube of side \(L\) are | \(E_{ijk} = \frac{4 \hbar^2 \pi^2 (i^2 + j^2 + k^2)}{2mL} = \lambda \frac{2 \pi^2(i^2 + j^2 + k^2)}{L}\) where \(\lambda=\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\) and \(i,j,k = 0,1,2,3...\) but \(i=j=k=0\) is not allowed. | The expectation value of the energy is | :math:`frac{1}{Z}sum_{ijk} left< Psi_{ijk}|hat H exp[-beta hat H]|Psi_{ijk} right> = frac{1}{Z}sum_{ijk} E_{ijk}exp[-beta E_{ijk}] ` where :math:`Z=sum_{ijk} left< Psi_{ijk}|exp[-beta E_{ijk}]|Psi_{ijk} right> `.

This sum will converge rapidly to \(2.51~K\) and can be computed numerically with a few lines of Python code, for example.
